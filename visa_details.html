Visa Requirements

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Flight booking details
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Proof of sufficient funds

Documents by Visa Category

For Job Holder
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from Employer (with an official Cell phone and TnT numbers).
  • Mandatory official ID card copy.
  • Salary Certificate/Payslip last 03 months (if Savings Account).
  • BMDC certificate for Doctor.
  • BAR Council Certificate for Advocate.
  • GO required with English translation and notary for Govt. employees.
For Business Persons
  • Renewal Trade license copy with notary public (English Translated).
  • Memorandum for Limited Company.
  • Office Pad / Company Letter Head Pad.
For Student
  • Student ID card copy.
  • Birth Certificate (Only for Child & infant).
  • Travel Letter or Leave Letter from the Educational Institute.
Other
  • Marriage Certificate Copy or Nikahnama (If spouse's name is not in passport).
  • Retirement certificate for Retired Person.
